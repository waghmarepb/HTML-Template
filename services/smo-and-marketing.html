While SEO and other forms of marketing will allow you to ‘sell’ your product or service, Social Media Optimisation (SMO) gives brands the opportunity to actually talk to their clients and forge relationships with them. SMO provides brands with the a chance to tell their story – how did the brand come into being, what does it believe in, how does it want to grown, how much it cares about its people – both employees and customers.
